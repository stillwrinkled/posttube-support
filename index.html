Frequently Asked Questions

How does PostTube work?
Simply paste any YouTube URL into PostTube. The app fetches the video transcript and uses AI to generate platform-optimized posts for LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Threads, Facebook, Email, and provides relevant hashtags. Each post is tailored to match the tone and style of its platform.
What videos can I use?
PostTube works with any public YouTube video that has captions or transcripts enabled. The video must not be private or age-restricted.
How many free generations do I get?
Every new user gets 3 free URL generations. No signup required, no credit card needed. This lets you test the full quality of PostTube before deciding to purchase.
What are the pricing options?
After your 3 free generations, you can choose:

Unlimited Subscription:

• Cancel anytime
Can I restore my purchases?
Yes! If you've purchased credits or a subscription, tap "Restore Purchases" in the paywall screen. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.
Do credits expire?
No. Purchased credits never expire and are tied to your device. Use them whenever you need them.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel anytime through your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
What platforms are supported?
PostTube generates content for: LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Threads, Facebook, Email newsletters, and provides suggested hashtags. Each post is optimized for its specific platform's audience and format.
Why did my generation fail?
Common reasons:
• Video doesn't have captions/transcripts enabled
• Video is private or age-restricted
• Invalid YouTube URL format
• Network connection issues

Try a different video or check your internet connection.
How do I get a refund?
All purchases are processed through the App Store. For refund requests, visit reportaproblem.apple.com or contact Apple Support directly. Include your order number and reason for the refund request.
